Welcome to our website!

This project is born out of a simple yet powerful idea: What if the intriguing stories of Vikram and Betal could be explored through the lens of the Bhagavad Gita’s teachings?

Each story on our website brings you a classic Vikram-Betal tale — rich in wit, moral dilemmas, and ancient wisdom — followed by a reflection that draws parallels with the spiritual and philosophical insights from the Gita.

Whether you're a fan of folklore, a seeker of wisdom, or someone simply curious about how two iconic parts of Indian heritage can come together, we hope this space sparks thought, reflection, and a bit of wonder.
