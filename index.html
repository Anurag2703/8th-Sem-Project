Welcome

The stories revolve around the legendary King Vikramaditya and the ghostly spirit Betaal, who resides in a corpse hanging from a tree. Each night, as Vikram attempts to capture Betaal and carry him away, the spirit tells him a gripping story that ends with a riddle or moral dilemma. Bound by a condition that he must answer the question if he knows the answer, Vikram repeatedly solves the puzzles, causing Betaal to escape and restart the cycle.
